STANDARD GAUGE RAILWAY

This is a story map of Kenya's Standard Gauge Railway.
  • Tools used are a storymap builder
  • Links at storymaps.arcgis.com

This story map was a creation inspired by the fact that there was a great project being undertaken in Kenya. This is the construction of KENYA,S STANDARD GAUGE RAILWAY.This is a great proof of how Kenya,s transport sector is heading in the right direction.

The urge to share a story about this beautiful 'snake' grew and i set out to make a story map of it. The power of story maps is that you can use them in any way to tell any story which is more captivating to the readers eye as compared to simply writing an essay.click to view this story map.
